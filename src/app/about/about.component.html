About us

Lasarkali Consumables is an emerging multi-local company. The abbreviation ‘Lasarkali’ is a portmanteau or linguistic blend of the names of Hindu Goddesses Laxmi, Saraswathi and Kali who represent prosperity, wisdom, strength and creativity respectively. Headquartered in Hyderabad, Lasarkali Consumables is on a path towards becoming one of the most recognised household names under the FMCG industry in the country. The company strongly believes in upholding the trust, integrity and esteem of its valuable customers and this shows in the quality of our products.

Lasarkali Consumables’ range of products is widespread. A purchaser can find products suitable for all kinds of requirements starting from the beginning of the day until the end of the night. We believe in providing quality household commodities at competitive prices for the contemporary society. No stones are left unturned in the packaging of products, done with the best of standards and decorum. Our Mission is to triumph in providing the finest products without compromising on quality.

Our result-oriented team values diversity while working in an agile and efficient environment. We have a strong sense of responsibility towards the country and are committed to being a part of its rapid growth.

Lasarkali Consumables’ Vision is to touch the lives of billions of people in the country through our products and being a valuable asset to the Indian economy.
